## Route Map
- /

## Page Roles
- Home: introduce brand, show latest expertise, drive to contact
- About: background, skills, and trajectory
- Blog index: discover all published writing by tag
- Blog detail: full post reading experience with strong metadata

## Content Hierarchy
1. Hero and positioning
2. Recent posts as proof of expertise
3. Contact call-to-action

## Navigation Model
Top navigation: Home, Blog, About.
Footer: social links and copyright.
